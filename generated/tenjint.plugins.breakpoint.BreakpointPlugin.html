BreakpointPlugin¶

class tenjint.plugins.breakpoint.BreakpointPlugin[source]¶

Breakpoint Service

This plugin implements the Breakpoint service and is responsible for setting and removing breakpoints. The breakpoints are set on a specificfied GPA and are hidden by this service.
